Experiences

Young Technology Scholars

Selected for this prestigious tech summer program by Plaksha University from over 10000+ applicants across India.I learnt about Arduino and python connectivity and how Electromyography (EMG) is a technique which will help to make bionic limbs for humans. Developed a bionic arm which contracts or releases based on the threshold electrical signals sent by the non bionic arm. I was mentored by Harvard and Northeastern faculty who expanded my knowledge in the biotech industry and technological innovation. My team’s project was selected as the best project award out of 15 others and we presented it during the closing ceremony.

Inspirt AI 2022

Selected for the prestigious AI scholars online program taught by MIT and Stanford alumni out of 50,000+ applicants. Learned about machine learning and data analytics and applied the same in sentimental analysis of social media platforms. Collaborated with high schoolers from various countries and implemented AI models for computer vision and NLP applications using python and additional libraries.

Harvard Project for Asian and International Studies 2022 (HPAIR”22)

Selected out of 20,000+ applicants to attend the prestigious HPAIR India project. Attended 15+ talk sessions and learnt about money management,mental health, cinematography, medical industry and leadership. I personally communicated with entrepreneurs and got the opportunity to work with like minded individuals from Israel, US, Japan and develop solutions pertaining to “Misinformation and its effects”.

Student Council 2022

Appointed as the Vice Head Boy of the school to bridge the gap between students and teachers. Started the official Commerce Club of school and organized 10+ events ranging from interschools debates, quizzes (Quizathion and Quizzinius), corporate crisis management and many more. Motivated 200+ juniors and helped them to become a better public speaker and debater. Appointed as the President of literary club(2022-2023) and Vice President(2022-2023) of commerce club.
